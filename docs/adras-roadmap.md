# Adras 25-Upgrade Roadmap

This roadmap turns the "do all 25" vision into a phased product plan that can be shipped safely without breaking the operating core.

## Phase 1: Core Business Control

1. Public website polish for `adras.uz`
2. Smart onboarding from visitor to owner workspace
3. Workspace branding and business identity
4. Role-specific dashboards
5. Command center and operational inbox

## Phase 2: Owner Intelligence

6. Business summary / owner cockpit
7. Team admin center with owner controls
8. Shop and branch performance tracking
9. Product intelligence for top sellers and dead stock
10. Profit and margin visibility

## Phase 3: Inventory Depth

11. Inventory count mode
12. Barcode / QR stock workflow
13. Production planning
14. BOM and material intelligence
15. Notification center inside the app

## Phase 4: Commerce and Reporting

16. Customer and wholesale CRM layer
17. Reports center with strong filters
18. Print and export center
19. Activity and performance analytics
20. Universal search across the workspace

## Phase 5: Operating System Layer

21. Mobile-first PWA experience
22. Multi-language polish across the full app
23. Approvals system for sensitive actions
24. Daily close ritual
25. Telegram action loop with deep links

## Current Status

- Shipped foundation:
  - Command center
  - Automatic system tasks
  - Role-based task queues
  - Workspace and profile centers
  - Workspace activity log
  - Business summary

- Best next slices after this foundation:
  - Role-specific dashboards beyond the current owner summary
  - Branch performance drill-down pages
  - Product intelligence and profitability drill-down pages
  - Inventory count mode
  - Barcode / QR flows
